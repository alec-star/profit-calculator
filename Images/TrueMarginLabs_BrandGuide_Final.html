Design Rules

Non-negotiable guidelines that protect the brand and maximize impact.

âœ“ Always Do This

  • White backgrounds with emerald accents
  • Use emojis for icons and visual markers
  • Make achievements feel like wins (confetti, bold text, big numbers)
  • Bold, confident typography (800 weight for headlines)
  • Generous whitespaceâ€”let things breathe
  • High contrast text (black on white)
  • System fonts for reliability and speed
  • Social-shareable designs (screenshot-worthy)
  • Let the green punch through
  • Celebrate wins visually

âœ• Never Do This

  • Dark backgrounds (we're white-first)
  • Custom icons (use emojis or simple shapes)
  • Multiple accent colors
  • Thin or light typography
  • Cramped or dense layouts
  • Gray text on light backgrounds
  • Anything that doesn't scale cleanly on social
  • Decorative elements with no function
  • The green hidden or muted
  • Cute when you should be premium
